Solfood is a food intelligence studio. We combine strategy, law, data and innovation to guide companies, institutions and territories through every stage of their food journey — from research to market, from idea to impact.

We work where food meets complexity: territorial valorisation, AI-driven food innovation, policy design, market entry, legal certification, data management and narrative strategy. We don't just advise — we design, prototype and deliver.

Our clients range from agri-food companies and international corporations to universities, startups and public institutions. If food is part of your challenge, Solfood is your partner.

Antonio Caso Founder & Lead Consultant
The founder

Food historian.
Geographer.
European strategist.

Solfood was founded by Antonio Caso — food historian, development geographer and European project manager with 7+ years of experience at the intersection of Mediterranean food heritage, territorial strategy and EU-funded development.

Author of A tavola con gli Apuli (2019) and A tavola con le Aquile (2024) — two research volumes documenting agrifood heritage across Puglia and Albania. Secretary General of the Fondazione Dieta Mediterranea. His academic work has been presented at CIHEAM MedForum (Montpellier), published on Encyclopedia.pub, and cited in European food policy contexts.

He has designed and implemented 30+ projects across 18 countries, managed over €1M in EU grants, and trained 500+ professionals — building the field intelligence that underpins every Solfood engagement.

Our belief

The next competitive edge
will come from food intelligence.

In sport, the margin between winning and losing is measured in milliseconds — and increasingly in what athletes eat. In luxury hospitality, the most memorable experience is no longer a view or a thread count, it's a meal that tells a story. In corporate wellness, productivity doesn't start with software — it starts at the table.

Food is no longer just nutrition. It is culture, data, strategy and identity. The organisations that understand this first will move faster, attract better talent, build stronger brands and create deeper loyalty.

Solfood exists to give them that advantage.

Our method

How we turn
complexity into results.

01

Research & Intelligence

We start by understanding the territory — its products, its regulations, its market dynamics and its data. No strategy without deep knowledge. We combine field research, data analysis, stakeholder mapping and competitive intelligence.

  • Territorial & market analysis
  • Consumer & sentiment data
  • Regulatory landscape mapping
  • Stakeholder & ecosystem scan
02

Strategy & Design

We design the architecture of the solution — whether it's a brand, a curriculum, a market entry plan, a certification path or a data system. Every element is purposeful, coherent and built to scale.

  • Strategic framework design
  • Curriculum & content architecture
  • Brand & narrative system
  • Legal & policy roadmap
03

Prototype & Pilot

We test before we scale. Every strategy, product concept or communication system is validated through real contexts — with real clients, real markets and real feedback — before full deployment.

  • Pilot design & facilitation
  • Toolkit & tool development
  • Stakeholder testing & feedback
  • Iteration & refinement
04

Deliver & Scale

We deliver complete, transferable outputs — strategies, curricula, certified products, data systems, market plans. And we stay. Long-term partnerships, follow-up consulting and project design to sustain and scale what we build.

  • Full delivery & handover
  • Project & partnership design
  • International scaling
  • Long-term partnership

R&DFood Law
2022–2025

Albania DOP & IGP
National GI Prospection Study

A three-year confidential field study assessing the potential for Protected Designations of Origin and Geographical Indications across Albania. Seven supply chains were analysed in depth across the entire country — mapping products, producers, territorial identity and legal eligibility within the EU GI framework. One of the most comprehensive food intelligence studies ever conducted on Albanian agri-food heritage.

🌍 Albania · ✍️ Dr. Antonio Caso · 🔒 Reserved
